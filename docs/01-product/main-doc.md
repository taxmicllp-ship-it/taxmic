# Product Overview  

**TaxDome** is a cloud-based all-in-one practice management platform for accounting and tax firms【10†L729-L734】【15†L334-L342】. It consolidates CRM, document management, workflow automation, billing, and communication into a single system, replacing disparate tools (email, spreadsheets, file storage, e-signature) with one central workspace【10†L737-L744】【15†L407-L416】. The platform is aimed at tax preparers, bookkeepers, accountants, and professional services firms of all sizes. It supports customizable branding (portal URL, logo, colour scheme) and white-labelled native mobile apps for clients【15†L407-L416】. TaxDome is delivered as a SaaS solution (web app, plus Windows desktop drive and iOS/Android apps), with firm and client mobile apps and an optional desktop drive integration【18†L27-L34】【117†L28-L36】. Core product categories include: 

- **Firm Management:** Team collaboration, workflows (pipelines, tasks), reporting, and integrated apps.  
- **Client Management:** CRM (accounts/contacts, segmentation), secure client portal, and communication (messaging, email, SMS).  
- **Document & Workflow Automation:** Document storage with folder templates, customizable pipelines and automations (triggers/actions), client onboarding forms (organizers), and e-signature.  
- **Revenue & Billing:** Time tracking, invoicing (one-time/recurring), proposals, payment processing (via Stripe or CPACharge), and client payments portal.  
- **Client Experience:** Branded client portal and mobile app where clients log in to upload files, complete requests, sign documents, chat, view tasks, and pay invoices.  

TaxDome integrates with accounting and other software (e.g. QuickBooks Online, Xero, email providers, Zapier, payment gateways, scheduling tools) to synchronize data and streamline operations【104†L669-L677】【110†L710-L719】.

## Workflow Automation and Task Management  

- **Pipelines & Stages:** Firms define **Pipelines** to model their service workflows (e.g. “Tax Preparation”, “Bookkeeping”). A pipeline is a sequence of stages (e.g. Onboard → Prepare → Review → Deliver → Complete). Stages appear as columns on a Kanban board (Workflow > Pipelines). Each stage is assigned to specific roles or teams and can have automations attached.  
- **Jobs:** A **Job** represents an instance of work for a client (linked to an account) moving through the pipeline stages. When a client is onboarded or a recurring service is scheduled, a job is created in the pipeline. Jobs contain tasks, linked documents, and scheduling info. Staff can move jobs manually or automatically (e.g. when a document is signed).  
- **Tasks:** **Tasks (To-dos)** are discrete action items. They can be standalone (under a client’s account) or attached to a job. Each task has an assignee, due date, priority, and status. Tasks can have subtasks or checklists. Staff create tasks manually or via **Task Templates** (blueprints with preset assignee(s), timeline, and description). Templates can be triggered automatically in a pipeline stage (e.g. “Create Review Task” when job enters “Review” stage).  
- **Automation (Triggers, Conditions, Actions):** The automation engine allows configuration of triggers, conditions, and actions. Triggers include pipeline events (job created, moved to stage, stage time elapsed), account events (new client added), billing events (invoice paid), etc. **Conditions** let automations run only when certain criteria are met (e.g. account has a particular tag, custom field value, tax year). **Actions** can be: send an email/SMS (using templates with merge fields), create tasks or jobs (optionally assigning to account roles), request documents, apply tags, or move jobs to another stage. For example, a trigger “Job enters Stage X” might create a task for the assigned bookkeeper and email the client to upload missing docs.  
- **Task Assignment & Reminders:** Tasks can be assigned to roles or individual team members. If assigned by role, any team member holding that role on the account will receive it. Tasks appear in users’ **My Tasks** list and on the Calendar. Staff can comment on tasks or attach files. The system can auto-escalate or reassign overdue tasks. Clients can have tasks (client tasks) created for them via chat or organizers, which the client then completes (visible in portal as a checklist).  
- **Job Recurrence:** Recurring jobs can be scheduled (e.g. “Generate payroll reports every month”). TaxDome will create new jobs at scheduled intervals.  
- **Calendar:** A built-in calendar view shows all jobs and tasks by due date【94†L656-L665】【94†L707-L715】. It’s filterable by assignee, account, pipeline, priority, etc. Users can change start/due dates by dragging events. The calendar helps plan team workloads.  
- **Checklists & Pipeline Templates:** For common processes (e.g. year-end filings), firms build **Task Lists** with checkboxes within pipeline stages (these can appear as jobs with multiple tasks). Pipeline templates can be cloned for new clients or services.  
- **Integration with Docs & Comm:** Tasks and jobs link to documents; e.g. a “Prepare Tax Return” task may link to the uploaded organizer. Completing tasks can be made dependent on clients uploading docs or signing forms. Staff can create tasks directly from chat messages or document comments. Conversely, documents can spawn tasks (e.g. need to review after upload).  
- **Edge Cases:** If a pipeline stage is skipped (via automation), associated tasks may auto-cancel or reassign. If a client becomes inactive, pending tasks can be archived or reallocated. Job and task numbering, duplications, and merges are handled with warnings to avoid data loss.

- **Invoicing:** From the Billing module or within a client account, staff create invoices. An invoice can include service fees (fixed price) and time entries (billed hours) or both. When billing time, staff select unbilled time entries for the client and TaxDome generates invoice line items【97†L702-L711】【97†L729-L739】. Invoices are numbered automatically or manually, and can be saved as draft or sent. They can be one-off or scheduled from proposals.  
- **Recurring Invoices:** TaxDome supports **recurring billing schedules** for ongoing services【96†L658-L667】. When setting up a recurring invoice, the system reserves a block of invoice numbers and issues a fresh one each cycle【96†L675-L684】. Clients can opt to auto-pay via stored card (Stripe) or pay manually. Each installment is delivered as a one-time invoice. The schedule and history are viewable.  
- **Proposals & Engagement Letters:** Staff can draft proposals with services (one-time, recurring, or bundled), and present them to clients for approval. Proposals can be customized per client, include attachments (engagement letters), and support multi-tier pricing (e.g. “silver/gold/platinum” packages). Once a client signs, the agreed items can auto-generate jobs/tasks.  
- **Payment Processing:** TaxDome integrates with payment gateways. Firms connect **Stripe** (international) or **CPACharge** (US/Canada) from Settings【110†L665-L674】【110†L732-L741】. Through Stripe, clients can pay with credit/debit cards, Google Pay, and bank debits (ACH, SEPA, BECS, etc.)【110†L710-L719】【110†L726-L734】. CPACharge covers credit card/ACH for North America. The chosen processor becomes the default for client payments. When clients pay, TaxDome records the transaction and marks invoices as paid (partially or fully). Transaction fees can be set to be paid by the firm or passed to clients as line items.  
- **Payment Tracking:** All payments (online or logged manually) appear in the **Payments** tab (per client) and reconcile against invoices. Staff can issue partial payments, refunds, or write-offs. Automatic payment receipts and thank-you emails can be configured.  
- **Client Billing Portal:** In their portal, clients see a **Billing** section listing outstanding invoices (with due dates and pay buttons). They can review past invoices and payments. If set up, clients can store a card and enable autopay for recurring bills. Clients are notified by email/SMS when an invoice is ready and can pay via the link. The portal supports PCI-compliant card entry via Stripe【127†L515-L523】.  
- **Reminders & Late Fees:** TaxDome allows auto-reminders (email/SMS) for unpaid invoices using pipeline automations or the manual reminder tool. Late payment fees or service suspension can be automated.  
- **Tax & Compliance:** Tax rates (VAT/GST) can be applied per service. Invoicing templates comply with legal requirements (customizable for region).  

## Communication System  

- **Chat & Messaging:** The **Chat** module (Communications > Chat) provides secure messaging with clients【100†L659-L668】. Chats can be one-on-one or threads per topic. Staff can @mention colleagues to loop them into a thread. Chat messages support file attachments (drag-and-drop) and task requests (the “+To-do” button to add a client task list)【100†L668-L676】. When a client or team member posts, all participants and watchers get email/push notifications【100†L703-L712】. By default clients can start chats, but this can be restricted in settings. Firm members can send **internal chat messages** not visible to clients for confidential notes.  
- **Email Integration:** In Settings, admins can connect email accounts via IMAP/SMTP. Once connected, the **Email** module shows an integrated inbox for clients. Emails sent from TaxDome use the firm’s email address. Replies from clients appear in thread under the respective account. Staff can create and save email templates with merge fields (e.g. “Hello {{contact.first}}”). Email blasts to multiple contacts can be sent (with unsubscribe management). Email engagement (opens, clicks) is tracked. Automatic actions (via automations) can be triggered by email events.  
- **SMS Messaging:** With Twilio set up, staff can send SMS from the Communications > SMS page. Clients’ mobile numbers (from contact records) are used. SMS templates exist for common messages (e.g. “Your invoice # is ready”). Two-way SMS is supported (clients can reply to SMS threads). Notifications (e.g. job moved) can be sent by SMS. Clients receive SMS for multi-factor authentication if they use SMS 2FA.  
- **Notifications (Inbox+):** Every in-platform action (new chat message, file upload, completed task, etc.) generates a notification in **Inbox+**. The Inbox+ feed (top-right bell icon) aggregates alerts by category (Messages, Documents, Tasks, Team Mentions). Staff can follow accounts to receive all updates. Mobile app push notifications mirror Inbox+ for urgent events. Permissions determine who sees which notifications (only staff with access to that account).  
- **Notifications Customisation:** Each team member can filter their notification preferences (only assigned tasks vs all, choose SMS vs Email vs Inbox) under Settings【100†L722-L724】.  
- **Global Communication:** Firms can broadcast emails/SMS to segments (using tags or custom fields). A newsletter might be sent to all clients annually. The system tracks unsubscribes per contact.  
- **Client Communication Preferences:** Each contact can opt out of marketing (unsubscribe link) but still receive transactional alerts. Clients must approve their phone for SMS (GDPR/UK rules) before receiving reminders.  

## Client Onboarding  

- **Client Portal Setup:** Admins prepare the portal (branding, announcements) and optionally enable self-registration【101†L37-L45】. A batch import of clients can create accounts and send invitations.  
- **Automated Onboarding Pipeline:** Many firms use a dedicated “New Client” pipeline. When a lead becomes a client (via invite acceptance or a signed proposal), a job is created in the pipeline. Automated actions may send the client a welcome email with instructions, assign an internal “Set up client” task to staff, and schedule any initial appointments.  
- **Tax Organizers & Forms:** TaxDome includes customizable organizers (filing checklists). Clients fill these out online in the portal, with conditional logic (show/hide questions). Many organizers can be pre-built (e.g. business, individual) and edited per firm. Clients can upload documents directly into organizer questions.  
- **Document/Info Request:** Using the Requests features (checklists or file links), staff can solicit missing information from new clients. For example, an “Initial Info Request” might collect bank statements and ID documents.  
- **Portal Training:** Firms can send a sample item for signature or a “starter” chat to acquaint clients with the portal. An optional guided introduction or welcome video may be provided.  

## E-Signature  

- **Signature Requests:** Any PDF on TaxDome can be sent for e-sign. The sender drags signature fields onto the document (for client and/or staff) and sends an email. Clients sign in the portal or through a secure link【44†L714-L721】. The signed PDF is saved and appended with an audit stamp. Multiple signers and sequential signing is supported. Once signed, TaxDome can trigger further automations (e.g. move job to next stage).  
- **Status Tracking:** The document’s status shows “Awaiting Signature” or “Signed.” Staff can send reminders from the document view. Clients see a “Signatures” section in portal. Completed signatures can be bulk-downloaded.  

## Time Tracking  

- **Timer:** TaxDome includes a built-in timer. Users can start/stop a timer on any page, or manually create an entry. Entries record date, duration, description, and select a Service (for billing).  
- **Time Entry Management:** All time is logged under the Billing > Time Entries section. Entries default to “Unbilled” status. Staff review time entries before invoicing.  
- **Bill Time:** As described above, invoices can be created from time entries【97†L702-L711】【97†L729-L739】. There’s also a “Clear out WIP” tool to invoice all unbilled time for a client in one go. Time entries become line items on invoices, carrying their description and service.  
- **Internal Billing:** Firms can track non-billable time (for admin tasks, etc.) by marking entries as non-billable. Reports can separate billable vs non.  
- **Time Reports:** There are reports for unbilled hours per client, team utilization, and job costing. Teams can see who logged how much time and against which client.  

## Reporting & Analytics  

- **Dashboards:** The home screen (Firm Dashboard) can show key metrics: outstanding balances, upcoming deadlines, number of jobs at each pipeline stage, new clients this month, etc. Customizable widgets allow firms to monitor KPIs.  
- **Standard Reports:** Built-in reports cover billing (revenue by period, receivables aging), workflow (job counts and stage durations), tasks (open tasks by team), and time (total billed hours, WIP aging). Reports are filterable by date, client, team member, or tags.  
- **Pipeline Reporting:** Visual charts show pipeline throughput, bottlenecks, and average time in stage. AI/ML-powered forecasting (TaxDome AI) can project workloads. (E.g. “Work Forecaster” estimates how many staff hours will be needed to hit tax deadlines based on current pipeline.)  
- **Export & API:** While no public API, reports and lists (accounts, invoices, time entries, etc.) can be exported to CSV for further analysis.  

## Integrations  

- **QuickBooks Online:** As described, TaxDome links to QBO under Settings→Integrations. Invoices and payments from TaxDome sync into QuickBooks (one-way)【104†L669-L677】. The firm selects which TaxDome accounts sync as QBO customers. Future TaxDome transactions (invoices, payments) appear in QBO automatically. Sync errors (mismatched names or numbers) are reported for resolution. This keeps bookkeeping in sync.  
- **Xero:** Similar to QBO (details from TaxDome blog). Typically, invoices created in TaxDome sync to Xero as drafts, and payments record as receipts.  
- **Email Providers:** Office365/Gmail/IMAP sync is set up by connecting account credentials under Settings. Syncing allows sending/receiving from within TaxDome.  
- **Calendars/Schedulers:** Firms link external scheduler accounts (Calendly, Acuity, etc.) via Settings→Integrations【116†L445-L454】. Once connected, clients can book appointments in the portal or via shared link. Scheduled events appear in TaxDome (e.g. as tasks or calendar items), and the scheduler handles reminders. Team members can allocate slots for calls or meetings through the scheduler.  
- **Zapier (Apps):** TaxDome supports Zapier connections for any of hundreds of apps【108†L659-L668】. For example, a new TaxDome client (trigger) can create a contact in Mailchimp. Or a form submission in Google Forms (trigger) could create a new TaxDome lead. Since TaxDome has no open API, Zapier is the official integration route.  
- **Payment Gateways:** As noted, Stripe and CPACharge connection is done in Settings【110†L665-L674】【110†L732-L741】. TaxDome Payments (Stripe integration) can be used globally, CPACharge in US/CA. Both can process credit cards and ACH (with restrictions per country).  
- **IRS Transcripts:** Firms can connect TaxDome to the IRS Transcript Delivery System【114†L656-L665】. Once set up, staff go to a client’s Docs > IRS tab to request and download tax transcripts directly, instead of logging into IRS separately.  
- **Others:** TaxDome can integrate with some tax prep software via custom API (limited) or more commonly via Zapier. It also supports linking to cloud storage (Google Drive/Dropbox) for additional backups (via Zapier or custom setup).

Staff can also view client profile info and documents (but heavy edits are easier on desktop). The app supports QR code scanning of documents (to quickly upload to an account). Team members with admin rights can also invite new clients or adjust settings via the app.  

## Security & Compliance  

- **Encryption:** All customer data is encrypted at rest (including documents, emails, contacts, keys)【127†L523-L531】. Web traffic uses TLS v1.2+ (bank-level encryption)【127†L515-L523】. Email to/from TaxDome uses TLS too【127†L531-L540】. Payment card data is handled in PCI-compliant vaults【127†L515-L523】【136†L1-L4】.  
- **Authentication:** TaxDome requires password + email code for login by default【125†L674-L683】. Firms can optionally enforce two-factor authentication (2FA) using an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, etc.) for staff and/or clients【125†L679-L688】. SMS-based codes can be used as a backup. Admins can even mandate 2FA for all accounts, helping meet FTC or 7216 requirements【125†L679-L688】. Password strength and reset procedures follow best practices.  
- **User Roles & Permissions:** TaxDome uses a role-based security model. There are three system roles: **Owner** (full control), **Admin** (nearly full; can’t delete firm), and **Employee** (least privilege)【130†L1-L4】. Owners/admins define each employee’s granular **access rights**【134†L675-L684】【134†L722-L731】 (e.g. permission to Manage Accounts, Manage Docs, Manage Invoices, Manage Pipelines, Manage Team, etc.). Account-level roles are independent: a team member’s roles on accounts do not affect their system permissions【131†L699-L707】. By default, employees only see accounts they are assigned to; an access right “View all accounts” can override this.  
- **Audit & Logging:** TaxDome logs every significant action in the **Activity Feed** (jobs moved, invoices paid, files uploaded, role changes, logins, exports, etc.)【139†L683-L692】. Owners can audit who did what (showing IP, timestamp, before/after values). Documents and tasks also have individual “Audit Trail” logs. The Activity Feed is searchable and filterable by entity and date【139†L743-L754】.  
- **Compliance:** TaxDome’s infrastructure is SOC2/GDPR-ready and HIPAA-compliant【127†L489-L498】 (data centers in the USA are under AWS BAA). It undergoes regular penetration testing and PCI scanning【136†L5-L10】. Security policies include rate-limits on login codes, account lockouts after failed attempts, and forced re-authentication for IRS transcripts due to IRS rules【114†L671-L680】.  
- **Data Protection:** Daily backups enable point-in-time recovery (loss <5 minutes)【127†L480-L488】. Firms retain full control of their data; a top-up balance mechanism protects against unexpected firrm deletion【127†L504-L513】.  

## Different Plan Features

- **Essentials:** Core features – CRM (unlimited contacts), Client Portal (custom branding), Document management, Basic pipelines and tasks, Invoicing (one-time) without recurring or proposals, Email & Chat. Time tracking and basic reports included. Limited automation (simple stage automations). 2FA optional.  
- **Pro:** Adds on advanced features – Multi-level teams and roles, Recurring invoices, Proposals/Engagement letters with service tiers, Workflow automations (full triggers/actions), Checklists/Organizers, SMS messaging (via Twilio), Zapier integration, more report types, and client app branding.  
- **Business:** All Pro features plus high-end capabilities – Unlimited pipelines & automations, IRS transcript integration, advanced AI analytics, multi-currency support, additional payment integrations, API access (via Zapier) at scale, and dedicated account manager. Larger client limits and priority support.
